Economy and Trade

On this page

    Emeralds

    The plains composter is the only source of emeralds. Right-click the composter with a crop to convert the crop into emeralds.

    No other team can generate emeralds.

    Traders

    Each biome has one trader. Right-click to open a trade menu.

    BiomeTraderSells
    PlainsVillagerCrops, food, farming tools
    OceanVillagerAquatic goods, water-related items
    MountainVillagerOres, ingots, mining tools
    NetherPiglinNether materials, fire-resistant gear

    Traders are protected and cannot be killed.

    Trade flow

    1. Plains converts crops to emeralds at the composter.
    2. Plains trades emeralds to other teams for resources.
    3. Other teams spend emeralds at their own or another biome's trader.
