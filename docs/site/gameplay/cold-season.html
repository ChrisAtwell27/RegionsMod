Cold Season

On this page

    A 15-minute Cold Season begins every 15 minutes. First Cold Season starts at 15:00.

    Global effects

    • PVP toggles on. Permanent from Cold 2 onward.
    • Mountain Blood Tribute timer starts.

    Per-biome effects

    Ocean

    Water not adjacent to a heat source within 1–2 blocks freezes. Water without a nearby heat source damages any player standing in it.

    Nether

    Lifeline furnace burns fuel twice as fast.

    Plains

    Crops stop growing unless within 1–2 blocks of a heat source.

    Mountain

    Ore deposit cooldowns increase. Some deposits flood or freeze and become temporarily inaccessible. Blood Tribute timer is active.

    Blood Tribute

    Runs for the full 15 minutes of each cold season.

    Satisfied by:

    • Any enemy kill by a mountain player, anywhere on the map.
    • Any enemy death anywhere inside the mountain biome, regardless of cause.

    Not satisfied by:

    • Killing teammates.
    • Deaths of mountain players.

    If the condition is not met by the end of cold season, every living dwarf loses 1 life.
