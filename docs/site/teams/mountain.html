Mountain

On this page

    Lifeline: Blood Tribute

    A 15-minute timer runs during each cold season.

    Satisfied by:

    • Any enemy kill by a mountain player anywhere on the map.
    • Any enemy death anywhere inside the mountain biome, regardless of cause.

    Not satisfied by:

    • Killing teammates.
    • Deaths of mountain players.

    If the timer expires without the condition met, every living dwarf loses 1 life.

    Passive

    None.

    Cold season effect

    • Blood Tribute timer is active.
    • Ore deposit cooldowns increase.
    • Some ore deposits flood or freeze and are temporarily inaccessible.

    Trader

    Villager. Sells ores, ingots, and mining tools.

    Mass event

    If mountain permanently loses half its starting members (rounded down), a Warden spawns at the preset Warden spawn point. The Warden has 100 HP, never aggros dwarves, and attacks any non-mountain player in range. See Mass Events.
