Plains

On this page

    Lifeline

    None structural.

    Defense is economic. Plains is the only source of emeralds on the map. Other teams must trade with plains for currency.

    Passive

    None.

    Cold season effect

    Crops stop growing unless within 1–2 blocks of a heat source.

    Emerald generation

    Right-click the composter with a crop to convert the crop into emeralds. Only the plains composter works.

    Trader

    Villager. Sells crops, food, and farming tools.

    Mass event

    If plains permanently loses half its starting members (rounded down), three independent Pillager patrol squads spawn inside the biome and attack any non-plains player. See Mass Events.

    See Plains Classes.
