Join a game

On this page

    Find a game

    Look for a sign starting with [Nations]. It shows the current join count.

    Join

    Right-click the sign. Right-click again to leave before the match starts.

    When the minimum is reached, you teleport to the lobby automatically.

    In the lobby

    You spawn with a compass. Right-click it to open a 4-slot team picker.

    SlotTeamColor
    1OceanBlue
    2NetherRed
    3PlainsGreen
    4MountainGray

    No biome can hold more than 1/4 of total players. Overflow is redistributed.

    Match start

    1. Teleport to team spawn.
    2. Pick one of 5 classes.
    3. Timeline starts.

    You have 3 lives. At 0 lives, spectator mode for the rest of the match. Spectators can teleport to any living player.
