RegionsMOBA is a Minecraft MOBA-style minigame. Ocean, Nether, Plains, and Mountain each play by different rules — asymmetric lifelines, passives, and economies — on a single shared world. Matches run at least 60 minutes with alternating warm and cold seasons.

Asymmetric lifelines

03 / WIN CONDITIONS

Each team has a different structural weakness. Break it, and the team's players are on borrowed time.

Ocean

Conduit nexus

200 HP. Each successful break by an enemy removes 1 HP and respawns the block. At 0 HP, every ocean player's next death is final.

Nether

Furnace must burn

If the furnace goes out, all nether players get Weakness and take 1 heart every 30s. Furnace damage cannot kill — floor of 1 heart.

Plains

Emerald monopoly

No structural lifeline. Defense is economic: plains is the only source of emeralds. Other teams must trade with plains for currency.

Mountain

Blood Tribute

A 15-minute timer runs every cold season. Satisfied by any enemy kill by a dwarf, or any enemy death inside the mountain biome. Miss it — every dwarf loses a life.

Twenty-four classes

04 / KITS

Each team has 6 classes tuned to its biome economy. Click any card to flip for ability detail.

Systems to know

05 / GAMEPLAY
Every 10 min

Trial Chamber

A fixed arena at the map center where all four biomes meet. Waves fire on a 10-minute cadence; the team with the most kills claims the loot.
Half losses trigger

Mass Events

When a team permanently loses half its starting players, a one-time regional event fires: Warden, Wither, Elder Guardians, or Pillager patrols.
Plains-only

Economy & Trade

Emeralds come from the plains composter only. Every biome has a trader. The whole market routes through plains.
Registered

Ore & Mob Deposits

Registered blocks and spawn points with regen cooldowns. Outside deposits, blocks behave normally.
Operator

Commands

Area, target, position, match, and debug commands. Everything needed to configure a match and troubleshoot live.
[Nations]

[Nations] Signs

Place a sign, set a minimum, and let players right-click to join. Join count displays live on the sign.
