# Teams

Four teams. Each plays differently.

## Comparison

| Team | Lifeline | Passive | Cold-Season Effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ocean | 200-HP conduit. Permadeath at 0. | Permanent Water Breathing | Water freezes. Drowned swarm the biome. |
| Nether | Furnace must stay lit. Damage floor of 1 heart. | Permanent Fire Resistance | Furnace burns 2x faster. Cold water damages Nether players. |
| Plains | None structural. Emerald monopoly. | None | Crops stop without heat source |
| Mountain | Blood Tribute each cold season. | Permanent Night Vision | Deposits slow and flood. Tribute active. |
