# RegionsMOBA

Fabric minigame mod for Minecraft 1.21.11. Four asymmetric nations compete on a shared map.

The mod is dormant by default. A game starts when an admin places a `[Nations]` sign and enough players right-click to join.

## Teams

| Team | Lifeline |
| --- | --- |
| Ocean | 200-HP conduit. At 0 HP, next ocean death is final. |
| Nether | Furnace must stay burning. Damage floor of 1 heart. Permanent fire resistance. |
| Plains | No structural lifeline. Sole source of emeralds. |
| Mountain | Blood Tribute each cold season. Miss it and every dwarf loses a life. |

## Cold Season

A 15-minute Cold Season starts every 15 minutes. Water freezes. Crops stop. Furnaces burn twice as fast. PVP toggles on. After two cold seasons, PVP stays on for the rest of the match.

## Lives

3 lives per player. At 0, spectator for the rest of the match.
